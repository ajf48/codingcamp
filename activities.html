Schedule

Here at Aristotle Coding we have a busy schedule. Campers will wake up at 8am for breakfast, and then start each day by choosing one of over twenty differnt activites offered. Lunch is sevred at 1pm, and then campers select another activity. By 6pm campers will eat dinner and then have the rest of the night for free time until they go to bed at 10pm.
